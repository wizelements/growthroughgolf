Golf Lesson Plans

Ages 4–6 — Swing Basics Through Play
  • Beginner: Introduces playful movement, basic golf grip, and hand-eye coordination using animal walks and foam sticks.
  • Intermediate: Adds stance development, color-coded target games, and chipping fundamentals.
  • Advanced: Reinforces rhythm and swing tempo through guided drills and peer feedback activities.
Download PDF
Ages 7–9 — Posture and Precision
  • Beginner: Builds awareness of grip, posture, and alignment through visual cues and stretch drills.
  • Intermediate: Focuses on swing mechanics, chipping technique, and accuracy-based putting.
  • Advanced: Introduces consistent ball striking and target selection, with guided correction loops.
Download PDF
Ages 10–12 — Form, Focus & Flow
  • Beginner: Establishes full swing awareness, alignment, and swing-plane exploration.
  • Intermediate: Incorporates distance control, cone-based simulations, and posture drills.
  • Advanced: Adds pressure-based games, video feedback, and mental cue reinforcement.
Download PDF
Teens (13–17) — Power and Mental Game
  • Beginner: Refines setup, impact awareness, and swing consistency.
  • Intermediate: Develops strategic shot selection, course navigation, and tempo pacing.
  • Advanced: Engages in pressure-play simulations, journaling, and confidence cycles.
Download PDF
Adults — Functional Golf for Everyday Players
  • Beginner: Focuses on comfort, posture resets, and consistent contact with slow-motion drills.
  • Intermediate: Emphasizes tempo, iron control, and situational play scenarios.
  • Advanced: Introduces performance tracking, specialty shots, and goal setting techniques.
Download PDF
Seniors — Smooth Swings & Longevity
  • Beginner: Promotes posture with chair-assisted drills and mobility routines.
  • Intermediate: Enhances swing feel through tempo guidance and visual aids.
  • Advanced: Practices distance consistency and integrates guided visualization.
Download PDF
