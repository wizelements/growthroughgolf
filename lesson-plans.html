Lesson Plans

Structured sessions for all age groups

Ages 4–6: Swing Basics Through Play

  • Warm-Up: Animal walks + balance beam stance
  • Skill Focus: Holding the club, swing shape with foam sticks
  • Game: Target toss with soft balls
  • Reflection: “What was fun today?”

Ages 7–9: Posture and Precision

  • Warm-Up: Dynamic stretches + club yoga
  • Skill Focus: Short chip shots, swing rhythm
  • Game: Hit the hoop challenge (accuracy rings)
  • Reflection: “What helped you improve today?”

Ages 10–12: Form, Focus & Flow

  • Warm-Up: Jog + mobility flow
  • Skill Focus: Full swing control, directional aiming
  • Game: Par 3 simulation (cones and scoring)
  • Reflection: Goal setting for next practice

Teens (13–17): Power and Mental Game

  • Warm-Up: Resistance band swings, tempo drills
  • Skill Focus: Distance control, pre-shot routines, strategy
  • Game: 3-club challenge or mental focus scrimmage
  • Reflection: Journal one breakthrough or takeaway

Adults: Functional Golf for Everyday Players

  • Warm-Up: Joint mobility + posture resets
  • Skill Focus: Swing consistency, body-friendly technique
  • Game: Tempo challenge + pressure putts
  • Reflection: “What feels better now than before?”

Seniors: Smooth Swings & Longevity

  • Warm-Up: Chair stretches + tempo breathing
  • Skill Focus: Smooth motion, posture alignment
  • Game: Slow-motion chipping contest
  • Reflection: “What part of your swing do you trust most?”
