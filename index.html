Teaching Philosophy

I believe golf is more than a sport—it's a reflection of life and growth. Like gardening, you reap what you sow. You don’t fail until you stop trying. My instruction emphasizes patience, effort, and character. I meet each student where they are and help them grow with intention.

Assessment Approach

I assess student progress using both observation and data, with a focus on:

  • Accuracy: Target precision and control
  • Correlation: How body movement aligns with results
  • Consistency: Repeatability of swing and posture
  • Confidence: Belief and focus under challenge

Life Skills Through Golf

Golf teaches emotional control, responsibility, and resilience. These qualities are integrated into each session through creative games, self-reflection, and intentional feedback.

“Golf is a quiet teacher. It teaches you to breathe, think, and try again.”

Transferrable Coaching Experience

As a gymnastics coach at KidsGymUSA, I work with children as young as walking age up to 12 years old. I bring movement education, energy, and structure to every lesson. I know how to build trust, maintain engagement, and teach through body awareness and rhythm—all of which enhance my golf instruction style.
