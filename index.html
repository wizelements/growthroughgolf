Welcome

Coach Solo blends dynamic instruction in golf and gymnastics with a deep commitment to youth development. As a lead instructor at KidsGymUSA, I bring structured movement, fun progression, and intentional coaching that empowers children of all abilities.

This site features my curriculum and lesson plans designed to build physical literacy, discipline, and joy in movement.

  • Play-Build-Perform™: Combining gymnastics fundamentals with skill-specific sports training.
  • Curriculum Design: Structured lesson plans aligned with physical literacy models.
