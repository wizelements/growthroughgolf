Welcome

Coach Solo blends dynamic instruction in both golf and gymnastics with a deep commitment to youth development. As a lead instructor at KidsGymUSA, I bring structured movement, fun progression, and intentional coaching that empowers children of all abilities.

This site features my curriculum and lesson plans for golf and other youth sports, designed to build physical literacy, discipline, and joy in movement.

Highlighted Experience

  • KidsGymUSA Coach: Teaching ages walking–12, focusing on strength, flexibility, coordination, and fun
  • Golf Instruction: Character-based golf coaching with play-based skill progression for ages 4–17 and adults
  • Play-Build-Perform™ Method: Combines gymnastics fundamentals with skill-specific sports training
  • Curriculum Design: Developed structured lesson plans aligned with First Tee and physical literacy models
